feminine sanitary disposal:

Twin P Ltd's sanitary disposal service is designed to help you provide an enhanced washroom experience to your female users. We provide you with white modern sanitary bins that are convenient to the users. Twin P Ltd's feminine hygiene units feature a wide-opening modesty tray, safely and hygienically hiding the refuse from view inside the unit.

All of our sanitary disposal units contain BIOCLEAN granules to sanitize and combat unpleasant smells without harming the environment.

Twin P Ltd's sanitary disposal service includes the rental our sanitary disposal bins including regular scheduled visits from our trained and uniformed staff that provide dependable and discreet servicing. All used bins are removed from site and replaced with fully sanitized re-serviced units.
